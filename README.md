# Minimum Viable Prompt (MVP)

🤖 **A single prompt that transforms any AI chat into a structured, reliable, personalised working session — in under 2 minutes.**

*No prompt form-filling. No technical knowledge. Just paste and go.*

---

## What is it?

The MVP is a ready-made session configuration prompt. Paste it into any AI chat and instead of starting cold, the AI will interview you — one question at a time — to establish your goals, tone, language, working style, and preferences before a single word of real work is produced.

No technical knowledge required. No form-filling. Just paste and go.

Drop the MVP prompt into any AI chat and it'll auto-pilot your setup as if you'd created your own in-depth system prompt and in under 2 mins your session will be cooking on gas! 🔥

---

## How to use it

1. Open [`MVP-prompt.md`](./MVP-prompt.md)
2. Copy the full prompt
3. Paste it into any AI chat (Claude, ChatGPT, DeepSeek, Gemini, Grok)
4. Press send — the AI does the rest

The AI will guide you through a short setup conversation, confirm your preferences, and activate eight operating rules for the session. The whole process takes under 2 minutes.

---

## What it sets up

**6 personalisation parameters:**
- Your session goal
- Your background and context
- Language variant and tone (UK or US English)
- Your writing style (optional — paste a sample to have the AI mirror your voice)
- Your preferred working relationship (collaborative, advisory, or creative)
- An optional persona for the AI to adopt

**8 operating rules, automatically activated:**
- Response length control (Brief / Balanced / Detailed)
- No speculation or hallucination — uncertainty flagged explicitly
- Facts treated as sacred; your prose treated with care
- Rewrite protocol — propose first, execute only with approval
- Step-by-step rhythm — no racing ahead without sign-off
- The /chaos toggle — creative mode on demand (try it)
- No filler, no padding, no performative enthusiasm
- Confirmation before any long or irreversible output
